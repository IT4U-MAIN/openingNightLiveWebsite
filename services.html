Service 01

Social Drip Campaigns

We build anticipation with strategic content drops across Instagram, TikTok, and local food communities. Behind-the-scenes chef content, menu teasers, and construction updates that make people feel like insiders.

Every post is designed to generate shares, build your following, and create FOMO before you even open.

Service 02

Creator Tastings

We connect you with the right food creators, influencers, and local tastemakers for exclusive pre-opening tastings. Not random influencers—curated creators whose audiences actually make reservations.

Each tasting is designed for content: optimal lighting, Instagram-worthy plating, and experiences that make creators want to post.

Service 03

PR Outreach

Direct relationships with food editors at Eater, Bon Appétit, local magazines, and dining newsletters. We pitch your story, secure features, and land the coverage that legitimizes your restaurant.

No agency bloat. Just targeted outreach to publications your ideal customers actually read.

Service 04

RSVP Landing Page

Custom landing page designed to convert interest into confirmed reservations. Early-access RSVP forms, countdown timers, social proof, and scarcity messaging that drives urgency.

We build the funnel, manage the waitlist, and deliver you a list of confirmed guests ready to show up.

Service 05

Street Promo & Activation

Guerrilla marketing in high-traffic areas: flyer drops, street teams, pop-up tastings, and local partnerships that create buzz beyond social media.

We bring your restaurant to where your customers already are—creating real-world awareness that converts to opening night attendance.

Service 06

UGC Content Capture

Professional content capture on opening night: guest reactions, dish highlights, kitchen action, and atmosphere shots that become your ongoing marketing library.

We also encourage and coordinate guest-generated content with hashtag campaigns and photo prompts that amplify your reach organically.

Service 07

Post-Launch Momentum

The work doesn't stop after opening night. We provide 30 days of momentum support: content repurposing, follow-up creator outreach, PR reinforcement, and ongoing RSVP management.

Our goal isn't just a packed opening night—it's sustained demand that keeps your restaurant full for months.
