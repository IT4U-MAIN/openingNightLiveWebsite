Case Study: Noma Dallas

The Brief

High-concept Nordic restaurant with Michelin-trained chef, zero local brand awareness, opening in competitive Deep Ellum district. Goal: sell out opening night and create sustainable demand.

Tactics

  • 30-day social drip campaign with behind-the-scenes content
  • Seeded 40 local food creators and influencers with exclusive tastings
  • PR outreach landing features in Eater Dallas, D Magazine, and Dallas Observer
  • RSVP landing page with early-access waitlist
  • Opening night production with street team and live content capture

Results

2,143 Total RSVPs
87% Show-Up Rate
5 Press Features
$87k Opening Week Revenue

Key Learnings

Creator seeding 3 weeks before launch generated the most authentic content. The RSVP funnel created scarcity that drove urgency. Post-launch, UGC from opening night continued to drive reservations for weeks.
