We Turn Empty Restaurants Into The Hottest Ticket in Town

Opening Night Live was born from a simple observation: the best restaurants fail not because of their food, but because nobody knows they exist.

Our Story

After watching too many talented chefs open incredible restaurants to crickets, we knew something had to change. Traditional restaurant marketing—basic social posts, paid ads, and hoping for the best—doesn't work anymore. The game changed.

Today's diners don't find restaurants through ads. They discover them through creators, influencers, and curated experiences. They want to be part of something exclusive, something talked-about, something worth posting.

So we built a system: creator seeding that generates authentic buzz, PR outreach that lands real features, RSVP funnels that create scarcity, and opening night production that turns launches into events people can't stop talking about.

Our Values

Taste First

We only work with restaurants we'd actually eat at. If the food isn't exceptional, no amount of marketing will save you.

Speed Wins

The window for a successful launch is tight. We move fast, execute flawlessly, and get you to sold-out status in weeks, not months.

ROI or Die

Every campaign we run is measured by one thing: did it fill seats? Vanity metrics don't pay rent. Revenue does.
